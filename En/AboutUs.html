About Me

I was born in 1983 in Prague, Czechoslovakia, and moved to Finland after high school. My coaching career began in 2003 when I started coaching a junior floorball team in my hometown. In 2012, I became a certified personal trainer (FAF Personal Trainer), and in 2023, I began physiotherapy studies at Metropolia University of Applied Sciences.

After becoming a personal trainer in 2012 through the Fitness Academy of Finland, I started working at Forever Hiekkaharju and later joined Fibaco, where I worked for 9 years. I founded my own business in 2017, renaming it Vacek Valmennus in 2022. That same year, I began my physiotherapy studies at Metropolia University of Applied Sciences.

I live in Myllypuro with my wife and three children, along with our two cats. My own training focuses mainly on calisthenics-style bodyweight exercises.

Testimonials

I have trained with Matej for about five years. During this time, I have come to know him as a smart, friendly, patient, and thoughtful trainer who genuinely listens to his clients and continuously seeks to improve himself. Matej puts real effort into designing the best possible training program for each individual. Since starting his physiotherapy studies, he has gained even more insight into coaching.

In training sessions, he pushes you to achieve the best performance you’re capable of at that moment—not more, but also not less. He always encourages you, especially when you feel like you didn’t do as well as you hoped. If you’re looking for a trainer whose sessions leave you going home with sore abs (either from laughing or from core exercises—sometimes both), but always in a better mood than when you arrived, Matej is the right choice!

I have learned that while it’s important to face the facts and be ambitious, focusing on strengths and positive reinforcement often leads to much greater progress than criticism ever could. And the best part? This lesson applies not just to training, but to life as a whole!
