Our Services

Initial Assessment & Background Evaluation

€59/h, one-time per client

The first step towards coaching: we assess your current fitness level and discuss your goals. The session can take place at a gym, your home, a café, outdoors, or remotely via Teams or Zoom.

Personal Training

€99/h

Personalized coaching based on your needs—strength training, mobility, endurance, or recovery. Sessions are held at Myllypuro's Liikuntamylly, Töölö's Kisahalli, or Itäkeskus swimming hall gym. At Liikuntamylly, we can also utilize the indoor running track.

Home Training in Helsinki

from €109/h, final price depends on location

If getting out of the house is challenging, I’ll come to you. I bring high-quality training equipment and can make use of your home environment effectively.

Assisted Transportation to Training Facilities

from €109/h, final price depends on location

If you need help getting to the gym, I can assist and ensure you get there safely.

Remote Training via Teams

€89/h

Perfect for frequent travelers or those living farther away. Sessions can be done without equipment or using what you already have at home.

Outdoor Training in East Helsinki

€79/h
